Nicola Rennie

Data Scientist

Nicola Rennie is a statistician and data scientist, passionate about using R to solve problems. She currently works as a Data Scientist at Jumping Rivers, having previously been a Mathematical Consultant at the Smith Institute. Nicola completed her PhD in statistics and operational research at STOR-i CDT at Lancaster University, where she researched the use of outlier detection in decision support systems in collaboration with Deutsche Bahn, Lufthansa, and Swiss Airlines.
